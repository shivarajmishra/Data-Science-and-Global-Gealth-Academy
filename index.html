Services

Our list of awesome services.

Academy for data science and global health is a research agency working in data-driven research for health discovery and innovation in low resource settings. It primary works in decision analytics, predictive modeling and artificial intelligence. The organisation was established in 2021 - with an aimbition to solving critical public health issues in low income settings. Since inception, dsAcademy has strived to conducting health and research activities, providing consulting support to developmental organisations and mentoring students and academics from low income settings. It is legally registered as a company under the Office of the Company Registrar, Ministry of Industry, Government of Nepal, having its central office in Chitwan, Nepal.

MetaNCD

This project aims to use traditional as well as advanced statistical techniques including generalized and mix effect models to explore geographical variations in disease and risk factors. This will be accompanied by geographical mapping and visualization which has the potential to inform policy makers.
